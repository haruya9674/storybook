Urashima Taro

Urashima Taro was a kind fisherman. One day, he saved a turtle on the beach. The turtle took him to the sea palace. Urashima stayed there and had fun. When he returned home, many years had passed. He opened a box from the turtle. Suddenly, he became very old.
