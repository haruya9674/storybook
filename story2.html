He loved to help people in the village. One day, Momotaro heard about scary ogres on a far island. He decided to go and stop them. On his journey, he met a dog, a monkey, and a bird. They became friends and worked together. With teamwork and courage, they defeated the ogres. Momotaro returned home safely and happily.
