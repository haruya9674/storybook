Momotaro

Long ago, an old man and an old woman lived in a village. One day, the old woman found a big peach in the river. Inside the peach was a baby boy. They named him Momotaro. Momotaro grew strong and kind. One day, he went to fight the ogres. On the way, he met a dog, a monkey, and a bird. They became friends and helped him. Together, they won the battle. Momotaro returned home safely.
